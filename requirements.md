# SPC Support for Roaming Authenticators - Requirements and Design Considerations
## Background
This document outlines the requirements for SPC support of [roaming authenticators](https://www.w3.org/TR/webauthn-2/#roaming-authenticators) based on issues raised in issue #[12](https://github.com/w3c/secure-payment-confirmation/issues/12)
and #[31](https://github.com/w3c/secure-payment-confirmation/issues/31)/[92](https://github.com/w3c/secure-payment-confirmation/issues/92)

## Terminology
The use of terms "MUST", "SHOULD", and "MAY" are defined in [RFC2119](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/html/rfc2119).
